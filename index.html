Why it inspires

Liquid surfaces instantly signal energy and curiosity. Blend subtle motion with purposeful storytelling to anchor visitors in your narrative, and punctuate the scene with typography that feels like captions on a cinematic sequence.

How to remix it

Play with displacement maps, metallic materials, and gentle camera drift. Swap textures or layer typography over the canvas to guide the eye, and align every content block to a focal point in the animation.

Share your ripple

Capture a screen, record a loop, or ship the effect in your next portfolio section. The more you experiment, the more fluid your storytelling becomes—pair each deliverable with short notes so viewers learn alongside you.

Learning path

Build the effect in layers

Treat each phase like a page in a playbook. Start with motion, inject light, then tell a story with typography and sound.

01 · Flow Foundations

Create the plane, wire uniforms, and ensure your camera easing feels organic.

  • Set up renderer + adaptive resize
  • Map cursor or scroll to displacement
  • Keep FPS capped for calm fluidity

02 · Material Drama

Push metalness/roughness values and blend HDRI lighting for curved reflections.

  • Experiment with normal maps + color LUTs
  • Balance bloom vs. clarity
  • Layer gradients in post-processing

03 · Story Layer

Translate emotion with copy, type rhythm, and supporting micro-interactions.

  • Use scroll snap to bookend scenes
  • Fade in stats, quotes, or case studies
  • Ship a short loop as a teaser

Hands-on lab

Remix this shader live

Drop the snippet into your sandbox, tweak one variable at a time, and note how the surface responds. Screenshot your favorite combo.

Try this flow

  1. Change displacementScale to exaggerate ripples.
  2. Animate metalness from 0.1 → 0.9 on scroll.
  3. Swap loadImage with your own displacement map.
  4. Capture the motion using the browser Media Recorder API.
// tweak.js
import LiquidBackground from 'threejs-components/backgrounds/liquid1'

const scene = LiquidBackground(document.getElementById('canvas'))
scene.loadImage('assets/noise-xl.png')
scene.liquidPlane.material.metalness = 0.4
scene.liquidPlane.material.roughness = 0.15
scene.liquidPlane.uniforms.displacementScale.value = 7
scene.setRain(true)
